**Understanding environmental drivers impacting species range shifts**
Climate and land use change are causing redistributions of species globally. These shifts are generally expected to occur toward higher latitudes and elevations; however, range shifts occur at a variety of rates and often occur in unexpected directions. Through this work, we examine factors that may contribute to these unexpected shifts, including impacts of land use on microclimate gradients and extreme weather and climate events.
